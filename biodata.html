Parcelia Renyelia

LET'S GET TO KNOW ABOUT ME!

Parcelia Renyelia Anak Pamg was born on October 1, 2000, and is a single Malaysian citizen. She is proficient in multiple languages, including Malay, English, Mandarin, Bidayuh, Iban, and Kayan. Parcelia holds a Diploma in Secretarial Science from Politeknik Mukah, Sarawak, and has completed her primary education at SK Jalan Muara Tuang, Kota Samarahan, and secondary education at SMK Muara Tuang, Kota Samarahan.

In her professional career, Parcelia has worked as a Library Assistant at the Sarawak State Legislative Assembly (DUNS) for almost three years. Her primary responsibilities involve providing reference materials and data to Honourable Members (ADUNS) and Political Secretaries, including Hansard, Gazette, Command Papers, Law Journals, and other legal books. Prior to this, she worked as an Administration Assistant at a construction company and volunteered at a Covid-19 Vaccination Centre.

Parcelia's skills include emceeing, singing, debating, and book cataloging. Her achievements include graduating with a 3.81 CGPA, being on the Dean's List every semester, and winning the Best Debater Award in 2019 and 2020. Additionally, she secured the 3rd Place in Polyskills Stenography in 2020. In her free time, Parcelia enjoys karaoke, reading books, attending motivational talks, and engaging in craft business.
