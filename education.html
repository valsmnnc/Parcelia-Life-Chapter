Parcelia's Education Background!

As a dedicated individual with a strong educational background, Parcelia is proud to highlight her achievements in both primary and secondary education. Her primary school experience was at Sekolah Kebangsaan Jalan Muara Tuang, Kota Samarahan, where she developed a solid foundation in various subjects. This school, known for its excellent academic performance, has a rich history dating back to 1964 when it was established to cater to the growing educational needs of the local community. With a strong emphasis on both Bahasa Melayu and Bahasa Cina, the school has consistently produced high-achieving students, including Parcelia, who have gone on to excel in various fields.

In secondary school, Parcelia attended SMK Muara Tuang, Kota Samarahan, where she further honed her skills and knowledge in a range of subjects. This school, like her primary school, is renowned for its rigorous academic standards and has produced many successful graduates who have gone on to pursue higher education and professional careers.

In addition to her academic achievements, Parcelia has also pursued further studies, earning a Diploma in Secretarial Science from Politeknik Mukah, Sarawak. This diploma program, which she completed with a remarkable 3.81 CGPA, has equipped her with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the field of secretarial science. Throughout her studies, Parcelia consistently maintained a high academic standing, earning a place on the Dean's List every semester. Her academic achievements were also recognized through several awards, including the Best Debater Award in 2019 and 2020, as well as the 3rd Place in Polyskills Stenography in 2020.

These achievements not only reflect Parcelia's dedication to her studies but also demonstrate her ability to excel in various fields and her commitment to continuous learning and self-improvement.

Parcelia, a diligent student who excelled in her Diploma in Secretarial Science program, was interviewed by TVS to share her remarkable journey of maintaining exceptional academic performance, earning the Dean's List every semester with a 3.81 CGPA. Her remarkable achievement is a testament to her unwavering dedication and commitment to her studies. When asked about her secret to success, Parcelia attributed her consistent performance to a well-balanced routine that included regular study sessions, effective time management, and a strong support system.

Parcelia emphasized the importance of setting clear goals and prioritizing her tasks to ensure that she stayed on track. She also highlighted the role of her academic advisors, who provided valuable guidance and mentorship throughout her program. Additionally, Parcelia credited her peers for their encouragement and camaraderie, which helped to foster a sense of friendly competition and motivation to excel.

Parcelia's academic journey was marked by a relentless pursuit of excellence, as she consistently pushed herself to achieve higher grades and maintain her high standards. Her unwavering commitment to her studies was evident in her ability to balance multiple responsibilities, including her academic workload, extracurricular activities, and personal life. This remarkable balance allowed her to excel in all aspects of her life, ultimately leading to her impressive academic achievements.

Parcelia's story serves as an inspiration to students who strive for academic excellence, demonstrating that with determination, hard work, and a supportive network, it is possible to achieve remarkable academic success. Her remarkable journey is a testament to the power of dedication and perseverance, and her achievements will undoubtedly serve as a source of motivation for many students to come.

Parcelia's ability to maintain her academic performance during the pandemic, when classes were conducted via Google Classroom and other online meeting apps, was particularly noteworthy. She recalled that it was not easy to consistently perform well under these circumstances, but she managed to stay focused and motivated through her routine and support system. Her ability to adapt to the new learning environment and maintain her academic momentum is a testament to her resilience and determination.
